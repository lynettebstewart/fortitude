Located in Oak Summit Office Park on the second floor of Building 200 (in Suite 210). Free parking provided in any of Oak Summit's parking spaces (please observe handicap parking designations).  

Once you're here

...come on up

I'm on the 2nd floor of Bldg. 200. If you have difficulty navigating stairs, just press the marked doorbell in the entry hall of Bldg. 200 for elevator/lift access.

...walk right in

The Suite 210 lobby door is usually closed. You're welcome to open it and have a seat in the lobby waiting area. During normal business hours Oak Summit provides a receptionist who can verify you are in the right place. You do not need to identify yourself to the receptionist if you do not wish to do so.

...& have a seat.

Feel free to grab a magazine while you wait, they're usually current and cover a variety of interests, they're just hard to spot unless you're looking for them. Check the large basket on the floor at the end of the lobby chairs.
