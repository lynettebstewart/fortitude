895 State Farm Road

Located in Oak Summit Office Park on the second floor of Building 200 (in Suite 210). Free parking provided in any of Oak Summit's parking spaces (please observe handicap parking designations). In winter this parking lot is privately maintained with salting & scraping.  

Once you're here

...come on in

The Suite 210 lobby door is usually closed. You're welcome to open it and have a seat in the lobby waiting area. During normal business hours Oak Summit provides a receptionist who can verify you are in the right place. You do not need to identify yourself to the receptionist if you do not wish to do so.

Accessibility

Building 200 is wheelchair accessible with elevator access available upon request. The elevator style is dated and runs loudly, but it is in good working order and it is used on a regular basis by staff, renters, & visitors. There is a buzzer in the ground floor entry of Bldg. 200 that you can press to obtain elevator access.

Food & Beverages

Fortitude Counseling keeps a stock of complimentary K-cups and bottled water so clients can always enjoy coffee, tea, bottled water, or even hot chocolate or cider in season. If you need a last minute snack or blood sugar boost, there are also food & beverage vending machines located at the back of the first floor of Bldg. 200.

Restrooms

There are restrooms available within Suite 210. One is located right off the lobby and if needed the receptionist can direct you to the other restroom located on the opposite side of the suite. 

Picnic Area

While you will see picnic tables around the parking area of the office complex, there is a private, peaceful picnic area immediately to the right of Bldg. 200. It is enclosed by shrubbery on three sides and clients are welcome to enjoy this area before or after appointments.
