Fees & Cancellation Policy

Payment due in full at time of service (MC, Visa, Check, Cash)

$ 150

1 hour session

This is generally the fee for a counseling session (unless it's your first one with me or you haven't seen me in a while-- in those cases, you should also check out the next box in this section).

30 min sessions cost $100

$ 200

initial session

This is the fee for a first session with a new client, or a session for a returning client who has not been seen in a while. The cost is more in part because of the substantial amount of paperwork and documentation required when someone starts / resumes counseling. 

$ 75

report / letter writing

Sometimes clients will request a detailed report or letter to be written for them. I offer this service at $75 PER HOUR of preparation time. Often we can determine an estimate in advance based on what you need and how long I think it will take to prepare it.

CANCELLATION POLICY: Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance, otherwise you may be charged a $75 cancellation fee if we are unable to find a time to reschedule in that same calendar week. Insurance & third party payers (like churches) cannot be billed for missed appointments which means the client (or legal guardian) is responsible for this fee.

I am unable to offer a sliding scale, client subsidy, or reduced session fees at this time.
