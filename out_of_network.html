How this works

You verify your out of network benefits.

I've made a one page, step-by-step pdf to walk you through the process. This will let you know IN ADVANCE what/if your insurance company will reimburse you for counseling.

We meet for our session(s).

You pay for your session in full each time we meet. Generally, this is $200 for the initial session, then $150/session after that.

Your insurance claim is filed.

An out of network claim is filed with your insurance company. This happens either by you submitting a "Superbill" I give you to your insurance company, or by you uploading the superbill from me to the app Better that submits the claim for you. Better does all the work for you and charges 10% of whatever reimbursement amount you're sent by the insurance company (no charge if you're not sent reimbursement money). Learn more about the Better app.

You get reimbursed.

Your insurance company reimburses YOU (usually by check through USPS mail) whatever they’ve agreed to pay for out of network services on your plan (this is the information you obtained in the very first step in this process).

Medicare Associated Policies

If you have an insurance policy associated with Medicare (usually these are related to age or disability status), you may want to go ahead and contact me (see below) so we can figure out your insurance situation together. The way Medicare policies work with other insurance policies in mental health can be complicated and it's often worth me taking a few extra steps on my end to make sure we're as clear on your benefits as possible before you arrive. So if you've got a Medicare associated policy, it's probably best to wait to do any benefits research until we talk and I can verify some things on the provider side of your insurance.
