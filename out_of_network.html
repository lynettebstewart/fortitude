Out of Network Insurance
How this works

You verify your out of network benefits.

Use this one page, step-by-step pdf to walk you through the process. This will let you know IN ADVANCE what/if your insurance company will reimburse you for counseling.

We meet for our session(s).

You pay for your session in full each time we meet. Generally, this is $200 for the initial session, then $150/session after that.

Your insurance claim is filed.

An out of network claim is filed with your insurance company. This happens either by you submitting a "Superbill" I give you to your insurance company, or by you uploading the superbill from me to the app Better that submits the claim for you. Learn more about the Better app.

You get reimbursed.

CANCELLATION POLICY: Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance, otherwise you may be charged a $75 cancellation fee if we are unable to find a time to reschedule in that same calendar week. Insurance & third party payers (like churches) cannot be billed for missed appointments which means the client (or legal guardian) is responsible for this fee. 

Unfortunately, I am unable to offer a sliding scale, client subsidy, or reduced session fees at this time.

Please Note: when I use the term "Tricare" in this website, I am referring to Tricare East which is a separate branch of Tricare than Tricare West or Tricare for Life. The statements I make only apply to Tricare East.

If you have an insurance policy associated with Medicare (usually these are related to age or disability status), you may want to go ahead and contact me so we can figure out your insurance situation together. The way Medicare policies work with other insurance policies in mental health can be complicated and it's often worth me taking a few extra steps on my end to make sure we're as clear on your benefits as possible before you arrive.
